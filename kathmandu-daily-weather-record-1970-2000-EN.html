Kathmandu daily weather record 1970-2000

As I watch BBC news report on COP20, I started searching for the historical climate data in Nepal. I found a couple of sites from where the climate data could be extracted including WorldClim, but for quick data extraction National Climatic Data Center (NCDC) came handy. NCDC has record just for Kathmandu, unfortunately no other places in Nepal.

Downloaded the data and bit of Google Refine and ready to display.

According to available data (from January 1971 to December 2000), 36.6 was recorded in May 7, 1989 and -3.5 was recorded on January 11, 1978 which are the highest and lowest in the range respectively.
