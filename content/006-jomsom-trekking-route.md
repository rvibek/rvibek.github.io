For the past 10 hours, I had been reading tutorials on [Spreadsheet Mapper 2.0](http://earth.google.com/outreach/tutorial_spreadsheet.html) and [Fusion Tables](http://tables.googlelabs.com/Home) and the different ways to reproduce maps. Here is a [comparison](http://earth.google.com/outreach/tutorial_fusion_yourowndata.html#comparemappers) between two alternatives. While trying to reproduce Naya Pul to Muktinath (Jomsom Trekking Route) downloaded from [activityworkshop.net](http://www.activityworkshop.net/" target="blank), I realise Spreadsheet Mapper 2.0 does not allow to create tracks whereas fusion table does - one major difference between two. I needed capability to create tracks especially for this map thus I carried on with Fusion Tables. Right now the table is very basic â€“ I shall add new fields and ballooning functions later.
